C O M B U S T I O N

Lookie lookie at my bookie,
What lies within is many a goodie.
Guess correctly and be alright,
but be wrong and face satan's might.

The thing I desire now lies beyond my reach
One day I may catch a glimpse at the beach
It resides at home, watching the lone star,
If I had the hours, I'd certainly visit by car.
They remind me of a speedy wagon, so strong and formed,
Perhaps one day it's my arm that is warmed!
That's all you get, now don't take too long!
By wasting time, your doom you simply prolong.
